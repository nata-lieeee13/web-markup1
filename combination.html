Combination Skin

Combination skin is known as having areas that are both oily ( by the t-zone) and dry or normal ( by the cheeks). With combination skin
you have to experiment with the products to see what works best for you. When using the routine provided you may want to play
around with the products before settling down into a skincare routine that works for you.

First Step: Cleanser (Use Morning and Night)

Toleriane Purifying Foaming Face Wash

Description: This cleanser will clean skin of impurities while
maintaining skin ph and skin moisture barrier.
Main ingredients: Thermal spring water, Niacinamide, and
Ceramide-3
Price: €14.99-17.99
Usage recommendation: Apply on skin with lukewarm water
and rub in circular motion. Then you should rinse of the cleanser
and tap the skin dry.

Cerave Hydrating Cleansing Cream

Description: A hydrating cleanser that removes impurities and other
dirt from the face.
Main ingredients: Ceramides, Hyaluronic Acid, Glycerin
Price: €13.45 - €25.95
Usage recommendation: Apply the cleanser to damp face and rub in
circular motion to the face and then rinse off after.

Second Step: Moisturizer (Use Morning and Night)

Charlotte's Magic Water Cream

Description: This moisturizer will provide hydrated, glowy, plumper looking skin
Main ingredients: Bionymph peptide, vitamins c & e, rosehip oil, hyaluronic acid,
frangipani flower extract, camellia oil, aloe vera, and shea butter.
Price: €95
Usage recommendation: Charlotte Tilbury created a facial massage technique to
apply this cream.
Magic Step 1: Starting from the lower cheek, sweep cream out & up. Tap fingertips
across cheeks avoiding eye contour.
Magic Step 2: Place fingers under cheekbones, then smooth outwards and upwards
along cheekbones.
Magic Step 3: Gently pinch chin with your fingers, moving up your jawline in one
sweeping motion.
Magic Step 4: Starting in the middle of the forehead, massage in circular motions
moving out to the temples. Pat any excess in with your ring finger

Estee Lauder soft creme/mask moisturizer

Description: This moisturizer boost moisture with just one application
and also strengthens the skin barrier. Not only is it a moisturizer but it
can also be used as a mask.
Main ingredients: Sweet Kelp, red algae ferment, and coconut water
ferment.
Free of skin irritants like parabens, phthalates, and sulfites.
Price: €45-13.50
Usage recommendation: Apply the moisturizer on your skin after
cleansing your skin. To use it as a mask apply a thin layer for 5 minutes
then wipe off with a tissue.

Third Step: Sunscreen (Only in the morning)

La Roche-Posay Anthelios UVMune 400 Oil Control Fluid SPF50+
For Oily and Blemish-Prone Skin

Description: A fluid sunscreen that can protect your skin from UVB,
UVA and ultra-long UVA rays.
Main ingredients: Vitamin E, Zinc, Glycerin
Price: €23.95
Usage recommendation: Apply all over your skin after face is clean and
hydrated, from the top of the forehead to the chin to protect your skin
from the sun

Bondi Sands SPF50+ Matte Tinted Face Lotion

Description: This sunscreen is ultra light weight and has a sheer tint that
gives a matte finish look. It has high UVA and UVB protection.
Main ingredients: Zinc Oxide , Titanium Dioxide, Silica, Vitamin E,
Octocrylene and Iron Oxides
Price: €16.99
Usage recommendation: Apply after moisturizer 20 minutes before sun
exposure and reapply every 2 hours.

Tips for Combination Skin

Look for products with these three ingredients:

  • Hyaluronic acid
  • Salicylic acid
  • Lactic acid
Gently exfoliate and deeply mosturize
Can use a clay mask on the t-zone (oily area) and apply moisturizing mask to the cheeks (dry area).
