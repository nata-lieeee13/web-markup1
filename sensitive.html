Sensitive Skin

Sensitive skin is characterized by having easily irritated skin by external factors, skin products, or other factors.This can also due to a
underlying skin condition. You may need to contact a dermatologist depending on your skins reaction.

Tips for sensitive skin

When using products use ones that are:

  • gentle
  • minimal ingredients
  • avoid harsh chemicals
Always patch test before trying a new product
